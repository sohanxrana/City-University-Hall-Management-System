Admission Eligibility

  • Regular Students of DIU
  • Minimum SGP: 2.5 in case of Readmission/existing students
  • No Accusation/ Conviction from University or Government

Hall Fees (Effect from Fall 2024)

Per regular seat cost is BDT- 1100/- (Per month). But mode of the payment will be semester wise and advance.

  • Registration fee: BDT- 2200/- (One time admission fee, not applicable in every semester)
  • Fee for students: BDT- 6600/- (for 6 month)

The Hall payment should be done during academic registration (beginning in the semester)
